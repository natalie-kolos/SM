A Letter from the CEO and Founder

With over 20 years in the dating industry, my intellectual acumen and intuitive insight set me apart. I’ve built a substantial database of singles and a vast network of connections. My ability to perceive people with clarity allows me to predict the outcomes of their connections accurately. I forecast the best matches and how relationships will unfold, offering guidance for maximum benefits and minimal losses in terms of time, resources, emotions, or reputation. Explore my success stories with timelines [here]. True happiness goes beyond money and personal goals. It’s about doing what you love and positively impacting the world. While pleasure is about receiving, happiness is about giving and making a meaningful difference in others’ lives. This approach energizes everyone around. I believe great work comes from loving what you do. My business is driven by passion and heart, and I would continue even if I never had to work again. It’s about how many lives you touch, not changing the entire world.

Loving deliberately

Our Commitment to Quality and Inclusivity

  • If we believe we are unable to find the perfect match for a potential client or if their expectations are unrealistic, we will not accept them as a client. We only accept clients we are confident we can help, ensuring a high success rate.
  • Our services are highly sought after, and many people are interested in becoming our clients. We limit our clientele to just 12 clients a year, ensuring each receives maximum personal attention and priority.
  • We do not discriminate based on age, ancestry, color, disability, national origin, race, religion, sex, or sexual orientation.
  • We do not work with legally married individuals.
